About IB Fragrance

Founded in Pretoria, South Africa, IB Fragrance has been providing luxury scents to discerning customers for over a decade. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the fragrance industry.

With 35 branches worldwide, we bring the finest perfumes and colognes from renowned brands directly to our customers. Our expert team carefully selects each product to ensure it meets our high standards of quality and authenticity.

Visit our flagship store at 129 Madiba Street, Pretoria Foundation, and experience the world of luxury fragrances.
